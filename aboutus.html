R & S CONSULTANCY

R & S Legal Consultants is a full service legal firm focused on rendering business friendly legal advice. The firm was founded in 2010. Ever since, the firm has been navigating complex legal issues for its diverse client base. To keep pace with the growth of its business and be ever present for its clients, the firm is spread across offices in the major economic centres of Mumbai, New Delhi, Bengaluru, Pune and Ranchi. The firm represents clients from all major industries and sectors. It provides clients with maximum value from legal counselling.
